Model Details:
The blood pressure classifier uses AHA/ACC 2017 Guidelines for deterministic classification of BP readings into 5 categories (Hypotension, Normal, Elevated, Hypertension Stage 1, Hypertension Stage 2/Crisis). It additionally computes Mean Arterial Pressure (MAP), Pulse Pressure (PP), and heart rate analysis for comprehensive cardiovascular risk assessment.

Validation Dataset: Framingham Heart Study (adapted) — 1,000 records  |  Classification: Deterministic rules + risk scoring  |  Accuracy: Near-perfect on guideline-based categories
