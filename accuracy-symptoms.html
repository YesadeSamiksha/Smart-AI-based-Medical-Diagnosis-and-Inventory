Model Details:
The Smart Symptom Checker uses a dual-engine architecture:

1. Gemini AI (Primary): Google's Gemini 1.5 Flash model analyzes symptoms in natural language, providing structured JSON output with primary conditions, differential diagnosis, risk assessment, and personalized recommendations.
2. Weighted Rule Engine (Fallback): A custom-built symptom-disease mapping system with 11 diseases and 26+ symptom categories. Each symptom carries a clinical weight (1-3), and disease scores are computed as weighted sums. Top 3 diseases are returned with confidence percentages.

Disease Coverage: 11 conditions (Heart Disease, Hypertension, Pneumonia, Asthma, Bronchitis, Flu, COVID-19, Diabetes, Gastritis, Thyroid, Arthritis)  |  Symptoms Mapped: 26 categories  |  Validation: 1,000 test cases
