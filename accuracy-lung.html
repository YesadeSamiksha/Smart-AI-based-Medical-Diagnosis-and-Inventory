Model Details:
The lung cancer risk screening uses a USPSTF-aligned Clinical Scoring Algorithm with weighted risk factors. It evaluates smoking history (heaviest weight), pack years, age, symptoms (chronic cough, chest pain, dyspnea), unexplained weight loss, and family history. The model calculates a score out of 35 points and stratifies risk into Low/Medium/High categories.

Training Dataset: NLST (National Lung Screening Trial) adapted — 1,000 records  |  Test Split: 80/20 stratified  |  Cross-Validation: 5-fold
