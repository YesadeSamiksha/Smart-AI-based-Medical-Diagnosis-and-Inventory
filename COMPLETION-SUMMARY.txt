🎯 WHAT THE SYSTEM DOES:

User Flow:
  Patient → Selects symptoms (e.g., fever, cough, fatigue)
         → System calculates disease scores
         → Ranks top 3 diseases with confidence %
         → Recommends medicines with dosages
         → Shows disease-specific precautions
         → Displays follow-up questions

Example Output:
  Primary: Flu (85% confidence)
  Top 3: Flu, COVID-19, Common Cold
  Medicines: Paracetamol, Oseltamivir
  Risk Level: Medium
  Precautions: Rest, hydrate, monitor fever
