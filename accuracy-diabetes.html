Model Details:
The diabetes risk prediction uses an Enhanced Clinical Algorithm with sigmoid probability scaling. The model evaluates 15+ risk factors including age, BMI, family history, lifestyle factors, and 7 symptom indicators (each weighted by clinical significance). The scoring system maps to a 30-point scale, then applies a sigmoid function for realistic probability distribution. Validated on 1,000 patient records with cross-validation.

Training Dataset: Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset (extended) — 1,000 records  |  Test Split: 80/20 stratified  |  Cross-Validation: 5-fold
